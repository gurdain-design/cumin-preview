SCALFORD HALL HOTEL

The Pavilion is located within our beautiful landscaped gardens. Our marquee is complete with water and electricity supply and offers the opportunity to host large events within a picturesque country house setting. Our pavilion can host up to 500 guests, depending on set-up, and is the perfect venue for weddings, gala balls, product launches and functions of all kinds. Scalford Hall Hotel also boasts 77 beautifully presented en-suite bedrooms and two suites, meaning that customers do not have to worry about accommodation for their guests. We also have car parking available for 200 cars.
